🎂 5-Ingredient desserts Recipes

Quick and tasty desserts made with just 5 ingredients!

🍓 Yogurt & Granola Parfait

Crunchy, creamy, and delicious snack!

🛒 Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • ½ cup granola
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • ½ cup mixed berries
  • 1 tsp chia seeds

📝 Instructions:

  1. In a glass, layer Greek yogurt at the bottom.
  2. Add a layer of granola, followed by mixed berries.
  3. Drizzle honey over the layers.
  4. Sprinkle chia seeds on top.
  5. Enjoy immediately or chill for a refreshing snack!

🍌 Peanut Butter Banana Bites

Perfect energy-boosting snack with natural sweetness.

🛒 Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • ¼ cup dark chocolate chips
  • 1 tbsp crushed nuts

📝 Instructions:

  1. Slice banana into small rounds.
  2. Spread peanut butter on one banana slice and top with another to make a sandwich.
  3. Drizzle honey over the banana bites.
  4. Melt chocolate chips and dip each banana bite halfway.
  5. Sprinkle crushed nuts on top and freeze for 30 minutes. Enjoy!
