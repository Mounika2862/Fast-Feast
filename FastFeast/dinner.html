🍲 5-Ingredient Dinner Recipes

Delicious and easy dinner meals made with just 5 ingredients.

🍗 Garlic Butter Chicken

Juicy chicken cooked in garlic-infused butter.

🛒 Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp dried parsley

📝 Instructions:

  1. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken breasts and cook for 5 minutes per side.
  4. Drizzle with lemon juice and sprinkle parsley.
  5. Serve hot. Enjoy!

🧀 Cheesy Garlic Pasta

Creamy and cheesy pasta with a garlic kick.

🛒 Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked pasta
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 tbsp butter

📝 Instructions:

  1. Heat butter in a pan and add minced garlic.
  2. Add cooked pasta and stir well.
  3. Pour in milk and mix until combined.
  4. Stir in shredded cheese until melted.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!
